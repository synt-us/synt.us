Dreams

Dreams are a natural sequence of images that occurs during REM sleep, but they are more than just random images, and no, I'm not talking about stuff in dreams that "represents" something, I'm talking about higher dimensions. When you dream, do you ever feel like your head is affixed to something? For example, a monitor connected to a computer, and you're somehow controlling the computer. This is known as 2nd dimensional dreaming, there's an infinite amount of dimensional dreaming, so when you have "astral projections", you're touching reality even more than you would with dreaming. This is because when you dream, your visual cortex is overloaded with lots of information that attempts to link to other things that you remember. Precognitive dreams are very related to this, if you separate all the bullshit overload that the dreams are giving you, you get truth out of everything.

If we consider it, we could possibly kill ourselves in our dreams if we're able to grasp reality from ones own existing reality.
